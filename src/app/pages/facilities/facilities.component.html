TOOTH AND GUM CLEANING AND TREATMENT

The main objectives of gum disease and tooth treatment and cleaning are to promote reattachment of healthy gums to teeth, reduce swelling and inflammation, cleaning of the deep pockets, and reduce the risk of infection, and stop disease progression. The tooth is deep cleaned by an experienced Registered Dental hygienist and or NMC registered dentist. By having professional cleanings on a regular basis, you will help to protect and restore the health of your teeth and gums.
